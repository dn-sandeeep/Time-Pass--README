# <h1 align="center">TimePass: Friend Knows Best 🍿</h1>

**TimePass** is a professional, social-first Android application designed to revolutionize how we discover movies and TV shows. By moving away from generic, algorithm-driven ratings, it creates a dedicated space for trusted recommendations from your own "Inner Circle"—your friends, family, and specialized clubs.

## 🏠 Home Screen: Your Personal Social Feed
The Home Screen is the heart of **TimePass**, delivering a curated stream of movie and TV show recommendations from people you trust.



### Key Features:
- **Interactive Reactions**: 
  - **Like & Dislike**: Express your opinion on a friend's recommendation instantly.
  - **Comment System**: Start a conversation about a movie right on the post.
- **Content Moderation**: 
  - **Report Feature**: A robust system to ensure a high-quality and safe community environment.
- **Smart Utilities**:
  - **One-Tap Watchlist**: Save interesting finds directly from the feed without leaving the screen.
  - **Dynamic Filters**: Narrow down your feed by **Media Type** (Movies/TV), **Genre**, or **Streaming Platform** (Netflix, Prime, etc.).
- **User Engagement**:
  - **Suggestion Box**: Share your feedback directly with the developer from the Home screen.
  - **Search Friends**: Quickly find and connect with people using their `@username`.
- **Navigation**: Clean, intuitive **Bottom Navigation Bar** for seamless transitions between Home, Clubs, Watchlist, and Profile.

## 🛠️ Technical Excellence
Built with a modern, scalable architecture to ensure peak performance and a premium user experience.

### Technology Stack:
- **UI Framework**: [Jetpack Compose](https://developer.android.com/jetpack/compose) with **Material 3** components.
- **Core Language**: 100% [Kotlin](https://kotlinlang.org/).
- **Backend & Real-time**: [Firebase](https://firebase.google.com/) Core:
    - **Firestore**: Real-time DB for chats and social syncing.
    - **Authentication**: Secure Google & Phone Auth.
    - **FCM**: Real-time push notifications for activity.
- **Networking**: [Ktor](https://ktor.io/) for high-performance API calls.
- **Image Handling**: [Coil](https://coil-kt.github.io/coil/) for optimized, lifecycle-aware loading.
- **Architecture**: **Clean Architecture (MVVM)** with a robust Repository pattern for absolute separation of concerns.
- **Analytics**: [Microsoft Clarity](https://clarity.microsoft.com/) for deep insights into user experience.
